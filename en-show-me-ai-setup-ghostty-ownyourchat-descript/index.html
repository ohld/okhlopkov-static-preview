Show Me Your AI Setup #1: Ghostty, ownyourchat, Descript, Spokenly — Tools Review

AI tools review: Ghostty terminal, ownyourchat for syncing AI chats to SQLite, Descript for text-based video editing, Spokenly for local transcription on Mac.

Daniil Okhlopkov

27 Jan 2026 • 1 min read

I invited @og_mishgun to show his AI setup.

Tools Worth Trying

🟥 Ghostty — terminal for those too lazy to configure iTerm. Install and forget.
↳ ghostty.org

🟥 ownyourchat — Misha's project. Syncs all chats with ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity into a local SQLite database. Grep your AI conversations.
↳ github.com/mlshv/ownyourchat

🟥 Descript — video editing by editing text. Delete words — video gets deleted.
↳ descript.com

🟥 Spokenly — voice → text locally on Mac. Press Command, speak, get text. All on-device, free.
↳ spokenly.app
