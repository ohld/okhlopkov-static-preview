Privacy Policy for okhlopkov.com
Effective date: May 25, 2026

This policy explains how okhlopkov.com and the personal Pinterest Content Ideas integration handle data.

The Pinterest Content Ideas integration is intended for personal use by Daniil Okhlopkov to read his own Pinterest boards and pins for private visual reference workflows.

Data accessed:
- Pinterest account identifiers available to the authorized account.
- Boards, pins, pin metadata, descriptions, links, and image URLs available to the authorized account.

The integration does not request permissions to create, update, or delete Pinterest content.

Use:
Pinterest data is used to maintain a private visual moodboard, create local reference manifests, and select inspiration for newly generated article visuals. Source Pinterest images are treated as references, not as public assets for redistribution.

Storage:
Reference data may be stored locally or in private systems controlled by Daniil Okhlopkov. Raw Pinterest source packs, access tokens, and private manifests are not intentionally published in the public okhlopkov.com repository.

Sharing:
Pinterest data is not sold or shared with third parties. Final generated visuals and articles may be published on okhlopkov.com, but they are created as new content and reviewed before publication.

Contact:
Questions about this policy can be sent via Telegram at @danokhlopkov or through the public contact links on okhlopkov.com.
